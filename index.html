Down to Space: A Science Adventure

Have you ever looked down at the ground and wondered what's hiding underneath? Or looked up at the clouds and asked why they're shaped the way they are? Or stared at the night sky and thought, "What's actually out there?"

You're in the right place! This website takes you on a journey from deep underground all the way up to outer space. Along the way you'll learn about dinosaur fossils, the soil that grows your food, the sky and its weather, and the planets beyond our world. Everything is connected, and it all starts right beneath your feet.

How It All Connects

Here's something amazing: these four layers don't just sit on top of each other. They actually affect each other!

  • The minerals deep underground slowly break down into the soil that feeds our plants.
  • The soil soaks up rainwater that falls from the sky.
  • The sky gets its weather patterns from the sun, which is a star in space.
  • And the ancient fossils underground tell us how the whole planet has changed over millions of years.

Pull one thread and the whole web moves. That's what makes Earth science so fascinating!
